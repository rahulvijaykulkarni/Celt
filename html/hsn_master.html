HSN Master User Manually:-

    In the HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) master we enter the details of HSN code then enter the GST Rate of this HSN code and HSN Category.

  • Enter the following HSN Master details:

    • Enter “HSN Code”
      •
    • Select “GST Rate”
      •
    • Enter “HSN Category”
      •

  • >Click button to Save HSN master form.
    •
  • Use the Search Tab for Search the Particular HSN from the Grid View for making update information of HSN Master.
    •
  • Click On to display information in all the Fields.
    •
  • Click On button to Update information of HSN master.
    •
  • Click On button to Delete From HSN master.
    •
  • Click On button to Clear all information which entered in the information in HSN Master befor save.
    •
  • Click On button to Close HSN master.
