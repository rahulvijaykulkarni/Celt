Inventory Trasfer


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can Enter Inventory Transfer Information manually.

  • Enter the following Inventory Transfer Details:
    • Select " NEW " Button and Accordingly " Doc No. " will get generated.
    • Select " Date " from calender button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
    • Enter " Refrence NO. ".
    • Enter " Narration ".
    • Select " From Item " wich will be transfer .
    • Select " To Item " .
    • Enter " Quantity ".
  • Search Documents
    • Enter " Doc Number ".
    • Select " Doc Date " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Click On " + " shows the Inventory Transfer Details in Table.
  • Click On " GRID VIEW " to delete Displayed Record.
  • Click " SEARCH " button to Search Records.
  • Click " SAVE " button to Save Form.
  • Click " DELETE " button to Delete Form.
  • Click " CLOSE " button to Close Form.
