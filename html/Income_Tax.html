Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Income Tax Master manually.


Income Tax User Manual:-

    Here we find the Income tax Details of employee according to government rule and there Income.
  • Select "Gender"(Male, Female, Senior Citizen)
    •
  • Select "Search Income Tax (Year)"
    •
  • Click On Button for view the income Tax Information of employee
    •
  • Click On Button for add the new employee income Tax Information
    •
  • Select "Type"(Male, Female, Senior Citizen)
    •
  • Enter "Effective Since"(The Year which from make fill the Income Tax)
    •
  • Enter "Lower Value"(Start Amount of Income Tax Slab)
    •
  • Enter "Upper Value"(Up to Amount of Income Tax Slab)
    •
  • Enter "I Tax %"(Income Tax percentage for this Rang)
    •
  • Enter "Cess"(Cess Amount)
    •
  • Enter "Surcharge" (Surcharge Amount)
    •
  • Click onfor the Income Tax Information in the Income Tax Master.
    •
  • Click onfor the Income Tax Information from Income Tax Master.
    •
  • Click onfor Save the Income Tax Master.
    •
  • Click onfor Cancel the enter information in Income Tax Master.
    •
  • Click onfor Get this all record on Excel Sheet.
    •
  • Click onfor Close the Income Tax Master.
