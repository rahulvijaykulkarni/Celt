Payment History


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Payment History manually.


Payment History User Manually:-

    Using Payment History you will make Receipt and Payment entry in application. and upload the Ledger which come from client for make verification of bill and payment.

    Receipt:

      Here you will be make the Receipt entry of payment which come from the client.
    • Select "Received from"(Client, Other)
      •
    • If Select Client then Select "Client Name"
      •
    • Get "Company Name"
      •
    • Select "Bank Name"
      •
    • Get "Account Number"
      •
    • Select "Payment Against"(Invoice, Advance Payment)
      •
    • Select "Client Bank Name"
      •
    • Select "Client Account No."
      •
    • Enter "Amount"
      •
    • Enter "Received Date"
      •
    • Select "Mode of Transfer"(Payment transfer mode)
      •
    • Enter "UTR No"
      •
    • If payment make through Cheque then Enter "Cheque No."
      •
    • Click On button to insert receipt document in system.
      •

    Receipt Details:

      Here you will be get the Details of Receipt entry and make settled the payment against with Invoice of client.
    • Select "Client Name"
      •
    • Select "Payment Receiving Date"
      •
    • Select "Amount"
      •
    • Click On button to get invoice of the payment of selected date.
      •
    • Make Settled the invoice with the receiving payment by check on checked box of invoice
      •
    • Click On button to make Settled the Selected invoice.
      •
    • Make Adjustment in the wiht Amount
      •
    • Click On button to make save the Settlement of invoice.
      •

    Payment:

      Using this you will be make payment to employee and vendor.
    • Select "Paid to"
      •
    • Select "Transfer To"
      •
    • Get "Account No"(of Selected Bank)
      •
    • Select "Transfer from"
      •
    • Get "Account No"(of Selected Bank)
      •
    • Enter "Amount"(for Transfer)
      •
    • Enter "Payment Date"
      •
    • Select "Transaction Mode"(Cash / Bank)
      •
    • If bank then select "Mode of Transfer"(RTGS,NFT,Cheque)
      •
    • Enter "UTR NO."(For RTGS and NEFT)
      •
    • If select "Cheque"
      •
    • Then Enter "Cheque No."
      •
    • Enter "Cheque Received Date"
      •
    • Enter "Cheque Deposite Date"
      •
    • Click On button to get invoice of the payment of selected date.
      •
    • Make Settled the invoice with the receiving payment by check on checked box of invoice
      •
    • Click On button to make settled the Selected invoice.
      •
    • Make Adjustment in the wiht Amount
      •
    • Click On button to make save the settlement of invoice.
      •

    Sales Entry:

      Here you get the information about Received payment.

    Upload Ledger:

      Using this you will be make upload the Ledger of client in system.
    • Select "Client Name"
      •
    • Click On button to Insert the Ledger of selected client in system.
      •
    • Click On button to make process ledger in the system.
      •
    • Click On button to get Ledger Report from system.
      •

  • Click On button to Close the Payment History.
