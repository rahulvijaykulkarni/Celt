TDS Calculation


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter TDS Calculation details .

  • Enter the following TDS Calculation details:
    • Enter " Name".
    • Enter " Pan Card No".
    • Select " U/S".
    • Enter " Amount Paid".
    • Enter " Select Rate Of TDS".
    • Select " Date Of Payment " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
    • Select " Date Tds Deduction " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
    • Enter " Tds Paid Amount.
    • Select " Date Tds Payment " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
    • Enter " Challan No".
  • Click "New"Button to add new TDS Calculation record.
  • Click "Save"Button to Save TDS Calculation record.
  • Click"clear"Button to Clear the TDS Calculation record.
  • Click" Excel Output" Button to see excel sheet report.
  • Click on " close" Button to close TDS Calculation form.
