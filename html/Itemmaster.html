Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can Enter Item Information mannualy .

    • Enter " Item Name ".
    • Enter " Sales rate ".
    • Enter " Purchase Rate ".
    • Enter " Weight For One Product(Kg) ".
    • Enter " Weight For One Resource (Kg): ".

  • Click " DELETE " button to Delete records of item.
  • Click " EPORT TO EXCEL" button to get records in excel.
  • Click " CANCEL " button to the Form .
  • Click " CLOSE " button to the Form.
