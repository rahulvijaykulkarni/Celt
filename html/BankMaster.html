Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Bank Information manually.


Bank Information
  • Enter the following Bank Master details:
    • Enter " Bank Name ".
    • Enter " Branch Name ".
    • Enter " Address ".
    • Enter " IFSC code ".
    • Enter " Account Number of company ".
    • Enter " Opening Balance ".
    • Enter " Bank Type ".
  • Click " SAVE " button to Save Bank Master Form.
  • Click " EXPORT TO EXCEL " button to get records in excel.
  • Click " CLOSE " button to Close Bank Master Form.
  • Click " CLEAR " button to clear Bank Master details
  • Click " UPDATE " button to update Bank Master details
  • Click " DELETE " button to Delete Bank Master detaols
  • Click " Table " to display information in all the Fields.
