Grade Master


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Grade Master details .

  • Enter the following Grade Master details:
    • Enter " Grade code".
    • Enter " Grade Description".
    • Select " Reporting To".
  • Click "New"Button to add new Grade Master record.
  • Click "Save"Button to Save Grade Master record.
  • Click"clear"Button to Clear the Grade Master record.
  • Click" Excel Output" Button to see excel sheet report.
  • Click on " close" Button to close Grade Master form.
