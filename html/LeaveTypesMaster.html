Leave Master


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can Enter Leave Information mannualy .

  • Add leave type details:
    • Select " NEW " button and Accordingly " Leave Type ID " will get generated.
    • Select " Gender ".
    • Enter " Staff Leave Type ".
    • Enter " Staff Leave Description ".
    • Enter " Maximum No. of Leaves ".
    • Enter " Year ".
  • Click On " TABLE " shows details of Leaves in top of Enquiry Form.
  • Click " SAVE " button to Save records of leaves.
  • Click " UPDATE " button to Update records of leaves.
  • Click " DELETE " button to Delete records of leaves.
  • Click " CLEAR " button to Clear Enquiry Form .
  • Click " CLOSE " button to Close Enquiry Form.
