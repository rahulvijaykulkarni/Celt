Staff Salary Stucture


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Staff Salary Structure manually.


Staff Salary Structure User Manually:-

    Using Staff Salary Structure you will make designing the employee salary according to veriours heads.

  • Enter the following Staff Employee Salary details:

    • Select "Client Name"(It must be always Staff)
      •
    • Select "Unit Name"
      •

  • Earing Head:

      • Here enterd the information name of head and contribution in the salary.
    • Enter "Head Name"
      •
    • Enter "Percentage of Head in Salary"
      •

  • Lumpsum Head:

      • Here enterd the information about head which make fixed in the salary.
    • Enter "L_Head"
      •

  • Deduction Head:

      • Here enterd the information about head which make dedcuted from salary.
    • Enter "D_Head"
      •

  • Tax Deductions Head:

      • Here enterd the information about taxes which diducted form salary.
    • Enter "PF Employee"
      •
    • Enter "PF Formula Employee"(Employee PF Formula)
      •
    • Enter "ESIC Employee"
      •
    • Enter "ESIC Formula Employee"(Employee ESIC Formula)
      •
    • Enter "PF Employer"
      •
    • Enter "PF Formula Employer"(Employer PF Formula)
      •
    • Enter "ESIC Employer"
      •
    • Enter "ESIC Formula Employer"(Employer ESIC Formula)
      •

  • Click On button to Save Staff Salary Stucture.
    •
  • Click On button to Close Staff Salary Stucture.
