Sundry Payment Details


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can Enter Employee Payment Details manually.

  • Enter the following Payment Details:
    • Select " New " Button and Accordingly " CashSet " will get generated.
    • Select " Date " from calender button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
    • Select " Bank " .
    • Enter " Employee Code ".
    • Enter " Employee Name ".
    • Enter " Amount ".
    • Select " Head Code " .
  • Search Payment Details:
    • Enter " CashSet Number ".
    • Select " CashSet Date " from calender button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Click On " + " shows the Payment Details in Table.
  • Click On " GRID VIEW " Delete Details in Table.
  • Click " SEARCH " button to Search Payment Details in Form.
  • Click " SAVE " button to Save Payment Details in Form.
  • Click " UPDATE " button to Update Form.
  • Click " DELETE " button to Delete Form.
  • Click " CLOSE " button to Close Form.
