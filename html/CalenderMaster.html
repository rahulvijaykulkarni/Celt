Calendar Master


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Calendar Master Details manually.

Calendar Master
  • Enter the following Calendar Master details:
    • Click " Sr No " button " No " will auto generated.
    • Enter " Year ".
    • Select " Month " and Accordingly " Number of days in a month " will get generated.
    • Select " Number of holidays in month ".
    • Select " Number of Weekly offs in month ".
    • " Number of Working days will auto generated "
    • Click " ADD " button to Save details in Calendar Master.
    • Click " UPDATE " button to Update Calendar Master.
    • Click " DELETE " button to Delete Calendar Master.
    • Click " CLOSE " button to Calendar Master.
    • Click " CLEAR " button to Clear Calendar Master details.
    • Click " Table " to display information in all the Fields.
