Customerwise Service Tax Details

Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Customerwise Service Tax Details.

  • Enter the following Customerwise Service Tax details:
    • Select " Customers code ".
    • Select " From Date " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
    • Select " To Date " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).

  • Click " SHOW " Button to see main report .
  • Click " CLOSE " Button to close Customers ledger Details.
  • Click " EXPORT TO EXCEL " to see in excel.
