Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Attendance Policy Master manually.


Attendance Policy Master User Manually:-

Using this Attendance policy master you will be make defended the company policy to employee attendance like working hour, In time, Out time, etc.,

  • Enter the following Attendance Policy Master details:

    • If You have any existing Policy then Select
    • Enter “New Policy Name"
      •
    • Enter “Start Date"
      •
    • Enter “End Date"
      •

  • IN/Out Time Details:

    • Enter the In Time of Employee
      •
    • Enter the Our Time of Employee
      •

  • Other Details:

    • Enter the “Working Hours”
      •
    • Select the Roll of employee in the Organization (Glatt, Third Party, Peace Rate)
      •
    • Select the Transaction method (Pay Sheet, Invoice Party)
      •
    • Select the Week Off(Sunday & 1st 3rd Saturday or Sunday & 2nd 4th Saturday)
      •
    • Enter the “Early In & Early Out time” of Employee
      •
    • Enter the “Let In & Let Out time” of Employee
      •
    • If you want any Reminder then select them (Daily, Weekly or Monthly)
      •
    • Enter the “Deduction Policy”
      •
    • Enter the “Short Hours”
      •
    • Select the “Gender”
      •
    • Select OT Applicable or Not
      •
    • Enter the “Minimum OT hours”
      •
    • Enter the “Maximum OT hours”
      •
    • Select the Approval required for OT or NOT
      •
    • Enter the Company Off day’s
      •
    • Enter the “OT Rate”
      •
    • Enter the “Remarks”
      •
    • Check the box Auto shift available or not
      •
