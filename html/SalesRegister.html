Sales Summary

Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can Show Sales Register Reports .

  • Select " From Date " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Select " To Date " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Select " Select Unit "
  • Click " SHOW " button to display sales register reports in Enquiry Form.
  • Click " CLOSE " button to Close Enquiry Form.
  • Click " SEND EMAIL " button to send email to valid id in Enquiry Form.
