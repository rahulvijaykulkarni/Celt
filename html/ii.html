Attandance History


Welcome to CeltPayroll. Using this page you can enter Attandance History details .

  • Enter the following Attandance History details:
    • Click "Admin"Button Admin Information Open.
    • Click "Employee"Button Employee Information Open .
    • Click on " close" Button to close Grade Master form.
    • After Click On Admin Button Admin Details Will Be Display
      • Select " Form Date " from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
      • Select " To Date" from calendar button For example(dd/mm/yyyy).
      • Select "Department "
      • Select "Grade"
      • elect "Employee Name"
      • Click "Submit"Button to Save Attandance History Details
      • Click" Excel Output" Button to see excel sheet report.
      • Click on " close" Button to close Admin Details.
    • After Click On Employee Button Employee Details Will Be Display
      • Select "Unit "
      • Select "Month"
      • Enter "Month"
      • Click "Submit"Button to Save Attandance History Employee record.
      • Click on " close" Button to close Employee Details.
