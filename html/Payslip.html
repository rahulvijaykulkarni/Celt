Pay Slip User Manually:-

    Using this we Generate the Salary Slip of Employee and Get the pendency Details of UAN, PF & ESIC and this detail’s directly send to Employee through Email.

  • Enter the following Pay Slip details:

    • Enter "Month/Year"
      •
    • Select "Client Name"
      •
    • Select "State Name"
      •
    • Select "Branch Name"
      •
    • Click On Button to Getting to Salary Slip of Employee.
      •
    • Click On Button to Get Details of UAN, PF and ESIC for Selected Month.
      •
    • Then Select the Employee Name
      •
    • Click On Button for sending mail to Employee Directly.
      •
    • Click On Button to Close the Payslip.
